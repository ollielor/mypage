About me

After having graduated as a German and Swedish teacher from University of Oulu/Finland in 2009, I started my career in lower secondary education in Oulu region and moved soon to Helsinki. During my time in Southern Finland, I have worked in the field of language education in several schools – mostly at upper secondary schools (the Finnish /lukio/ ) but also at lower secondary and elementary schools (/alakoulu/ and /yläkoulu/) and in non-formal adult education (/vapaa sivistystyö/).

As I became more and more interested in different kinds of learning apps, I decided to start full-time studies in software engineering at Haaga-Helia University of Applied Sciences in Helsinki [link to: haaga-helia.fi]. My aim is to become a software development professional who is able to combine educational insights with technical expertise.

By nature, I am approachable, persistent and passionate about learning new things. I enjoy working in teams as an effective and constructive team member but I am also capable of working on my own.

At the moment, I am looking for *an internship related to my ICT studies at [Haaga-Helia] (preferably in the field of learning-related ICT projects). Feel free to contact me if you got interested!
